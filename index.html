About Planet Fitness

Since opening our doors in 1995, our vision has been to become South Africa’s most loved health and fitness brand. Whether it’s bringing generators on board to ensure your workout is never disrupted or including free Wi-Fi – we aim to make every workout at Planet Fitness an awesome experience.

Three Brands, One Promise

We understand that South Africa’s needs are as diverse as its heritage, we strive to offer value for money to all our customers across our well-loved brands Planet Fitness, Planet Fitness Signature and JustGym.

We Keep it Fresh

In a world that is constantly evolving and changing, why should your workout be any different? At Planet Fitness, we offer exciting new training facilities and classes to keep things interesting – helping you find your balance and a better way of living.
